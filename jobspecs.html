Front End Developer

As a front end developer you will have a team of 4-5 whom you will need to communicate with. You will also need to have a strong sense of teamwork in order for the work to progress smoothly. You will also need to have experience in the following languages:
•CSS
•HTML
•JavaScript
•PHP

Back End Developer

As a back end developer you will have a team of 4-5 whom you will need to communicate with. You will also need to have a strong sense of teamwork in order for the work to progress smoothly. You will also need to have experience in the following languages:
•CSS
•HTML
•JavaScript
•PHP

Junior Web Developer

As a junior web developer you will be under the guidance of one of the Front End or Back End developers and you will need to make sure that you are able to follow their instructions quickly and promptly. You will also need to have some experience in the following languages:
•CSS
•HTML
•JavaScript
•PHP

Graphics Designer

The graphics design team has their own department and you may work with others if you wish but it is not as important and you will have the option to work by yourself. You will need to have some experience in Art & Design and a porfolio ready.
